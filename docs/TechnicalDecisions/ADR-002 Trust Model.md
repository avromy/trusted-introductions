# ADR-002: Trust Model

## Status

Accepted initially.

## Context

The platform is private and invite-only. Trust comes from community relationships, not public signup scale.

## Decision

Use an invite graph, steward oversight, audit events, and explicit contribution settings to preserve trust.

## Consequences

- Invitations must be accountable to an inviter.
- Stewards need review and moderation tools.
- Growth should be constrained by quality and safety.
